Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Undertone is a steganography tool that lets you hide text messages inside PNG images using LSB (Least Significant Bit) encoding. This page explains what happens to your data when you use it.

What we process

When you use Undertone to encode or decode a message, the image you upload and the text you enter are processed only to perform that operation. We do not require an account, and we do not ask for personal information such as your name or email address to use the core tool.

What we don't store

  • We do not permanently store the images you upload.
  • We do not permanently store the messages you encode or decode.
  • Files are processed for the duration of your request and are not retained afterward.

Server logs

Like most web services, our hosting provider may automatically log basic technical information (such as IP address, timestamps, and request paths) for security and debugging purposes. These logs are not used to identify individuals and are not shared with third parties.

Cookies

Undertone does not use tracking or advertising cookies. Any cookies present are limited to what's strictly necessary for the site to function.

Third-party hosting

This application is hosted on Render. Render may process technical request data as part of standard infrastructure operation, governed by Render's own privacy policy.

Changes to this policy

This policy may be updated as the project evolves. Continued use of Undertone after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be raised via the GitHub repository.
