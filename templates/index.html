The process
01

Upload a photo

Any image works. It's the vessel — its pixels have more room in them than you'd think.

02

Write what it carries

Type your message. It's woven into the image data itself, not attached or hidden in a filename.

03

Share the image

Send it anywhere images travel. It opens like any photo — the message only surfaces with the key.

Unchanged to the eye

The output image is visually identical to the original — no watermark, no artifact, no tell.

Local by default

Encoding happens on this server, not a third party. Your photo isn't stored longer than it takes to process.

Lossless by design

Output is saved as PNG, so every hidden bit survives — no compression artifacts to shake it loose.
